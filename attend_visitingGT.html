Visiting Washington DC

Getting to Washington DC

Airports around DC Distance to venue
Washington National Airport (DCA) 5 miles
Washington Dulles International Airport (IAD) 25 miles
Baltimore/Washington International (BWI) 30 miles

There are three main airports that serve Washington DC, DCA, IAD, BWI. Alternatively, if you prefer to enter the United States through New York city and then catch a train to get to Washington DC, there are three main airports: John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK), LaGuardia (LGA), and Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR).

You can also arrive to DC by train. Amtrak has frequent daily departures from major cities throughout the Northeast, Southeast, New York, and even Chicago. Trains arrive at Union Station, bringing you to the heart of DC, that also has its own Metro Station and easy access to taxis and ridesharing.
