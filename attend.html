Attend

Attend

SIGIR 2024 is an in-person conference. We have a set of measures to ensure a pleasant in-person conference for all participants. Here's what you need to know:
(1) The registration site is now live! Participants can now obtain invitation letters to begin their visa applications. Please note that attendance at SIGIR is open to all, regardless of whether you have an accepted paper.
(2) All accepted papers and posters must be presented in person. Presentation is a crucial aspect of conference publications. We anticipate that at least one author from each accepted paper or poster will attend the conference in person to deliver the content and address audience questions during the Q&A session. In exceptional cases, if authors are unable to attend, they may designate a proxy presenter to represent their work in person. The author may also have the option to present remotely from the proxy's labtop, with the proxy presenter show up in person in the room. If the author cannot be presenting, the proxy should present the work to the audience. It is the authors' responsibility to select a qualified proxy presenter, ensure they are properly trained, and confirm their understanding of the technical content. We urge authors to plan ahead and allocate ample time to find, train, and rehearse with their proxy presenters. Please note that even if a proxy presenter represents a paper, at least one author must register for the conference.
(3) A sub-committee has been established to manage in-person presentations. Our Integration chairs, Jaap Kamps and Liqiang Nie, will help coordinate presentations. If authors are unable to find a proxy presenter, the Integration Chairs will assist in finding one, as the last resort. We emphasize the importance of planning in advance and providing enough time to find, train, and rehearse with your proxy presenter.
(4) Video recordings of technical talks will be available on ACM DL or YouTube after the conference. This allows SIGIR participants who cannot attend in person to access the technical content at no cost.

Stay tuned for more information!
