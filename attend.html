Attend

SIGIR 2024 is a fully in-person conference. We have a set of measures to ensure a pleasant in-person conference:
(1) Early opening of the registration site so that participants can get visa letters early to start their visa applications. Please note that you do not need your paper to be accepted to attend SIGIR. All are welcome!
(2) All accepted papersâ€™ authors are expected to attend the conference in-person. We will not collect any pre-recording of videos from the authors. Instead, we will collect slides from you before your talk if you have an oral presentation slot.
(3) We have establish a subcommittee to arrange in-person presentations. They are our Integration chairs, Jaap Kamps and Liqiang Nie. The integration chairs will work closely to coordinating finding proxy presenters for authors if they really cannot make it to the conference, as a last resort.
(4) Video recordings of technical talks will be released on ACM DL or YouTube after the conference. SIGIRers who cannot attend in-person will be able to access these tech content post conference, free of cost.

Stay tuned for more information!
